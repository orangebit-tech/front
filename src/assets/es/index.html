INTRODUCTION

Electrominer is a Blockchain Project founded in 2017 by Blockchain enthusiasts and Professionals in Green Energy Production, Construction, IT and Business. The project is created to address the problems of Blockchain Technology and to contribute to the future of decentralized technologies. Electrominer will push Blockchain potential in the right direction by creating sustainable, expandable and selfsufficient mechanism driven by community. We are inviting you to join us and build the future of decentralized technologies together!

Problems of Crypto-Mining:

-High electricity costs (45% of the mining reward)
-High level of CO2 emissions
-Challenges of hardware purchase and maintenance
-Long-term contracts provided by cloud-mining services
-Contracts’ attachment to a single coin/algorithm

Solutions offered by Electrominer:

-Self-powered sustainable mining facilities
-Use of alternative solar energy
-Professional 24/7 monitoring and maintenance of mining equipment
-Unlimited adjustable contracts
-Ability to swap between various coins/algorithms


DATACENTERS

Autonomous
Electrominer is building its own energy generating solar plant to minimize
electricity expenses and reduce negative effect of crypto-mining. Usage of
alternative energy will allow us to make mining process more cost-effective,
sustainable and autonomous.

Mobile
Electrominer Mobile Datacenter Units are based on shipping containers. Its
allows for their easy transportation and high level of mobility.

Reliable
Mobile Data Center Units will be equipped with an exclusively designed air
cooling system to solve the problem with overheating. Every unit will have
remote-control software which tracks the status of every processor
combining them into total computing capacity.

COULD MINING PLATFORM

Unlimited terms and flexible conditions
Electrominer, LLC offers unlimited, flexible and adjustable contracts
for self-increasing share of available hashing power. This will provide
our members with the opportunity to make even 1-hour long orders which can
be cancelled anytime. Users can also manage hashing capacity by spreading
it between multiple coins on multiple algorithms.

Multiplier
The Electrominer Platform will provide its members with the guaranteed
mining capacity and the floating multiplier. The multiplier will depend
on the number of tokens held and platform’s currents load as unused mining
power is spread between its active users. Therefore, since Electrominer
team is planning to increase its mining capacity through time, the amount
of guaranteed Hashrate per token will grow proportionally.

Low fees
Generation of our own solar energy will allow for low fees for renting
out hashing capacity.

MEMBERSHIP

Buying Electrominer Token means becoming a member of Electrominer Community. Members will get the access to the Mining Platform and receive mined value earnings with their guaranteed capacity. Electrominer will automatically transfer the value directly to users’ wallets. Electrominer will also collect a symbolic rental fee to provide token circulation which will allow new users to join the platform. Unused mining capacity will be distributed between active users according to the number of tokens held. Therefore, Electrominer will provide its members with an opportunity to participate in decisionmaking process of our management by taking part in voting on strategic improvements of our products.


CLOUD MINING PLATFORM

Our platform provides our members with guaranteed mining capacity available for rent and the floating multiplier. Mining capacity depends on the number of tokens held. The Web Portal and the Mobile Application will allow our users to track the status of mining progress and to withdraw funds anytime.
